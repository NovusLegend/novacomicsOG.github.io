The Idea

We sat down and thought of many things to do but we came to a conclusion that if we wanted to stand out, we should not start off with uperpowered heroes like superman, we should have a cordinating system and since this is heading to be a sort of marvel or dc kind we should have a system that is above all the other characters and keeps track of them, sort of the watcher and his infinite number of what-if universes that he monitors so we came up with something like that but in our own perspective

THE ARCHIVES®

THE ARCHIVES® are our first production.
It is a serie that is mainly about a giant library that consists of never ending books and each one of those books is a universe where once you turn a page foward, you view the future of that universe and vice versa.
THE ARCHIVES® were started by a god known as The Author and as time went by, the author found it was a waste of time for him to be the one to guard the ARCHIVES® so he appointed a series of spicies to become the keeper of the ARCHIVES®.
